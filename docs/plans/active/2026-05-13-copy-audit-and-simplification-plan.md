# Copy Audit And Simplification Plan

## Status

In Progress

## Owner

Copy

## Last Updated

## Checklist

- audit runner-facing copy across login, onboarding, home, workout, feedback, progress, body, and integrations
- identify technical or internal-language copy that does not help the runner act
- simplify labels, helper text, empty states, and status messaging without changing product logic
- keep terminology aligned with `docs/glossary.md`, especially `preview` and `saved mode`
- capture layout or hierarchy problems that should move to Designer rather than being forced through copy alone

## Exit Criteria

- highest-friction technical copy is reduced on the main runner-facing surfaces
- updated wording is clearer, shorter, and less system-internal
- any remaining hierarchy or grouping issues are handed off clearly to Designer
