# Plan Creation Visual Variety Regression QA

## Archive Note

This archive preserves the 2026-05-26 focused QA pass for generated-plan visual variety and
coach-authored feel. It is evidence history, not a current QA instruction.

## Final Outcome

QA passed for controlled beta. Running Coach also passed the sampled output for controlled beta, with
the important caveat that this was not a perfect pass and future support-day richness could still
improve.

Preserved evidence:

- QA report: `docs/process/hito-plan-creation-visual-variety-regression-qa-2026-05-26.md`
- Screenshot folder: `docs/process/screenshots/plan-creation-visual-variety-regression-2026-05-26/`

## Key Decisions

- The risk being tested was not whether plans could be created; it was whether visible plans looked
  repetitive, under-structured, or non-coach-authored after opening the saved plan.
- The pass sampled multiple runner profiles and goal families, including beginner, supported 10K,
  half marathon, low-support marathon, relaxed ultra, and mountain/trail.
- Calendar screenshots alone were insufficient; workout-detail screenshots needed segment
  structure, cues, targets, and purpose text.

## Non-Blocking Caveats

- Beginner/easy support runs can still be intentionally plain.
- Marathon and ultra calendar views can look quieter because credibility may live in detail pages.
- Future evidence should include stronger mid-cycle calendar views for marathon and ultra.
- Support-day detail richness should continue improving over time.
