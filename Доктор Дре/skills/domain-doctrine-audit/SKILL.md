# Domain Doctrine Audit

## Purpose

Evaluate whether accepted product outputs satisfy domain quality and safety without taking over
technical implementation.

## Read First

1. product contract and accepted system truth;
2. domain doctrine, policies, or external standards where applicable;
3. representative outputs and evidence;
4. known product and technical constraints.

## Workflow

1. Define the review question and evidence boundary.
2. Inspect representative accepted outputs, not only fixtures or intentions.
3. Classify findings as blocker, improvement, unknown, or outside scope.
4. Identify the correct product or technical owner for each actionable finding.
5. Preserve the distinction between domain judgment and implementation mechanics.

## Rules

- Do not claim technical validation from domain review.
- Do not invent product rules without evidence or authority.
- State uncertainty and sample limitations plainly.

## Output

Task, Stage, Evidence reviewed, Doctrine findings, Acceptance decision, Recommended owner, Blockers.
